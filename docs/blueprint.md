# **App Name**: PoultrySense AI

## Core Features:

- Image Upload & Analysis: Allows farmers to upload images of affected poultry for automated AI-powered diagnostic assessment.
- Symptom Description Input: Provides a user-friendly interface for inputting written descriptions of poultry symptoms.
- AI Diagnostic Tool: Utilizes a generative AI tool to process image and text inputs, providing potential diagnoses.
- Diagnosis Results Display: Clearly presents the AI's diagnosis, including confidence levels and identified issues.
- Recommended Next Steps: Offers practical, actionable next steps and treatment recommendations based on the diagnosis.
- Diagnosis History: Saves a history of past diagnoses and recommendations for easy farmer reference.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary green: #14B84A. A vibrant, yet professional green evoking health and growth, contrasting cleanly against the light background.
- Background color: #F0F5F3. A very light, subtly green-tinted white to maintain a clean, expansive, and professional aesthetic, in line with the 'glowy white' request.
- Accent color: #C8E052. A bright, analogous yellow-green that provides a refreshing pop for interactive elements and highlights, adding to the 'glowy' feel.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif). A modern, highly legible typeface that projects a clean, objective, and professional demeanor, suitable for conveying critical diagnostic information.
- Use minimalist, outline-style icons with rounded corners to maintain a clean and approachable professional appearance.
- Employ a grid-based layout with ample whitespace to ensure information is easy to scan and not overwhelming for practical farmer use.
- Implement subtle transition animations for diagnosis loading and results display to enhance the feeling of responsiveness without distracting from information.
